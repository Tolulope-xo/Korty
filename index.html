Eniola Stella-Maris Olanrewaju

About

Eniola Stella-Maris Olanrewaju (born 2 April 1998), better known as Korty EO, or simply Korty, is a Nigerian filmmaker and YouTuber known for her video series "Flow, With Korty". EARLY LIFE Olanrewaju was born in Southern Nigeria. She is a middle child; she has an older brother and a younger sister, Ralia. Korty graduated from the University of Ibadan in 2018, with a degree in computer science. She then relocated to Lagos for a job at Zikoko. Korty signed with Few Models and started modelling in 2017. She has walked the ramp for Lagos Fashion and Design Week and GTCO Fashion Weekend and modelled for Lisa Folawiyo's SS18 Kwenu collection. In March 2018, Korty began to work as a video producer for Zikoko before working for Mr Eazi's emPawa Africa as Head of Content in January 2020. She quit in December 2020 to focus on. Korty joined YouTube on January 18, 2019. She first started uploading videos in July 2020. As of November 2021, her channel has gained over 80 thousand subscribers. She began her video series "Flow, With Korty" in November 2020 and has since interviewed various celebrities like Don Jazzy, Ayra Starr and Blaqbonez.
